About us

Our story

Our company, Calculator Solutions, specializes in providing high-quality calculators to small businesses and individuals. We understand the importance of having reliable and efficient tools to help with everyday tasks, and our calculators are designed to provide accurate results every time. With a wide range of options to choose from, including basic models and more advanced scientific calculators, we have something to meet the needs of every customer. Whether you need a calculator for simple calculations or more complex equations, we have you covered.

Calculator Solutions has a long and storied history. Our company was founded in the 1950s, and we began by selling mechanical calculators to customers. These early calculators were simple, but reliable, and they quickly became a staple in offices and homes across the country.

Over the years, we have continued to evolve and grow, adding new products and services to our lineup. We were one of the first companies to introduce electronic calculators, and we continue to be at the forefront of the industry, offering the latest and most advanced calculators on the market.

Today, Calculator Solutions is a trusted name in the world of calculators, and we are proud to serve customers all across the country. We are committed to providing high-quality products and excellent customer service, and we look forward to helping you with all of your calculator needs.
